Foreword about my interests and expertise

My formal training is split between metamorphic petrology, continuum mechanics, and applied statistics. My PhD research focused on quantifying aspects of subduction zone geodynamics with large numerical and empirical datasets. For example, I asked questions like: how do metamorphic reactions affect the mechanical nature of the interface between converging plates? How does mechanical coupling between plates relate to detachment and recovery of subducting oceanic lithosphere? How can surface heat flow observations be leveraged to infer the spatial scales and variability of deep dynamic processes?

My current research aims at micro- and macro-scale processes in mantle petrology and geodynamics. I also think a lot about developing and applying machine learning (ML) methods to geoscience problems.

Feel free to message me if you see anything interesting on my site along these lines.
